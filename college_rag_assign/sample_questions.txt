# Sample Questions for College Student Knowledge Assistant

## Student Handbook (student_handbook.txt)
- What is the minimum attendance required to sit for the semester exam?
- If I miss an internal test, can I request a re-test? What is the procedure?
- How do I apply for a bonafide certificate and how long does it take?

## Academic Calendar (academic_calendar.txt)
- When does the next semester start and when are midterms scheduled?

## Course Syllabus (course_syllabus.txt)
- What are the prerequisites for Data Structures, and which topics must I know first?
- Give me the official unit-wise syllabus for Operating Systems.

## Placement Guidelines (placement_guidelines.txt)
- Am I eligible for placements if I have 1 backlog? What is the policy?
- What documents are required for internship approval?

## Fees and Scholarships (fees_scholarships.txt)
- Which scholarships are available for 2nd-year students, and what is the eligibility?
- What is the late fee rule if I miss the semester fee deadline?

## Library Rules (library_rules.txt)
- What are library fine rules for late book return?
- How do I reserve a book online?
