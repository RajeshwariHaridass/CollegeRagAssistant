Student Handbook 2025

Attendance Policy:
Minimum attendance required to sit for semester exams is 75%. Students with less than 75% attendance will not be allowed to appear for the exams.

Internal Tests:
If a student misses an internal test due to valid reasons, they can request a re-test. The procedure is:
1. Submit a written application to the HOD within 3 days of the test.
2. Provide supporting documents (medical certificate, etc.).
3. Re-test will be scheduled within 2 weeks.

Fees and Scholarships:
Late fee for missing semester fee deadline is Rs. 100 per day after the due date.

Scholarships available for 2nd-year students:
- Merit Scholarship: CGPA > 8.5
- Need-based Scholarship: Family income < Rs. 5 lakh

Placement Policy:
Students with 1 backlog are eligible for placements, but must clear it before joining the company.

Library Rules:
Late return of books incurs a fine of Rs. 5 per day.

Bonafide Certificate:
Apply through the admin office portal. Processing time is 3-5 working days.
