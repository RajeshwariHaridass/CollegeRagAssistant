Placement & Internship Guidelines 2025

Eligibility for Placements:
- CGPA >= 7.0
- No active backlogs
- Completed at least 75% of the curriculum

Internship Requirements:
- Minimum CGPA 6.5
- Submit application form with resume
- Approval from department HOD
- Documents required: Resume, ID card, Bonafide certificate

Placement Process:
1. Register on placement portal
2. Attend pre-placement talks
3. Appear for aptitude tests and interviews
4. Accept offer letter
