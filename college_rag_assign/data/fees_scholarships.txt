Fees and Scholarships 2025

Semester Fee Structure:
- Tuition Fee: Rs. 50,000 per semester
- Hostel Fee: Rs. 30,000 per semester
- Mess Fee: Rs. 20,000 per semester
- Total: Rs. 100,000

Late Fee Policy:
- Rs. 100 per day after due date
- Maximum late fee: Rs. 5,000

Scholarships Available:
1. Merit Scholarship: CGPA > 9.0, Amount: Rs. 10,000 per semester
2. Sports Scholarship: For outstanding athletes, Amount: Rs. 15,000
3. SC/ST Scholarship: Based on government norms, Amount: Rs. 20,000
4. Need-based Scholarship: Family income < Rs. 3 lakh, Amount: Rs. 8,000

Application Process:
- Submit online application on student portal
- Attach required documents (income certificate, marksheets)
- Deadline: Last week of semester
