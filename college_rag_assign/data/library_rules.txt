Library Rules and Guidelines

Borrowing Rules:
- Students can borrow up to 3 books at a time
- Loan period: 14 days
- Renewal: Once for 7 days

Fines:
- Overdue books: Rs. 2 per day per book
- Lost books: Cost of book + Rs. 100 processing fee
- Damaged books: Rs. 50 repair fee

Lab Manuals:
- Available for all engineering labs
- Must be returned after lab session
- No borrowing allowed for manuals

Project Guidelines:
- Reference books available in project section
- Group study rooms available on reservation
- Digital resources accessible via library portal

FAQs:
Q: How to reserve a book?
A: Use the online reservation system on library website.

Q: Can I borrow books during holidays?
A: Yes, but return before semester starts.
